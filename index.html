--- layout: home title: TAFFO subtitle: Tuning Assistant for Floating-Point to Fixed-Point Optimization ---

TAFFO is an autotuning framework which tries to replace floating-point operations with fixed-point operations as much as possible.

Get Started with TAFFO

TAFFO is developed and maintained at HEAPLab, Politecnico di Milano in collaboration with NTNU.
